SEVERE WEATHER & NATURAL DISASTERS

Be Prepared for Anything

You will experience severe weather

Severe weather happens everyday. More than 500 people die each year due to severe weather, including tornados, earthquakes, hurricanes, and thunderstorms. Severe weather also causes millions of dollars in damage.

Educate yourself to protect you and your loved ones.

Prepare Early

Everyone experiences different natural disasters and severe weather. Here's a list of possible disasters and how to prepare for them:

  • Tornadoes
  • Earthquakes
  • Thunderstorms
  • Snow Storms
  • Flooding

Are you currently experiencing severe or life-threatening weather?

  • Stay calm and seek shelter.
  • If possible, communicate with friends and love ones to check for injuries.
